How this SIP return calculator works

This sip investment calculator online computes corpus month by month. It applies contribution timing, monthly compounding, annual step-up, and target planning to produce SIP maturity amount and wealth gain projections year by year.


Formula reference (without step-up): FV = P * (((1 + r)^n - 1) / r) * (1 + r), where P is monthly SIP, r is monthly rate, and n is total months.

Is SIP return guaranteed in India?

No. SIP returns depend on market-linked mutual fund performance. This tool gives projections based on your expected annual return assumptions.

What is step up SIP and why is it important?

Step-up SIP increases your monthly investment yearly by a selected percentage. It is one of the most effective ways to reach 50 lakh or 1 crore corpus targets faster.

How to calculate SIP returns in India for long-term goals?

Enter monthly SIP amount, expected return, and tenure. You can then test scenarios like sip calculator 5000 per month returns, 10-year SIP plans, and retirement goal planning.

What does inflation adjusted SIP corpus mean?

Inflation adjusted corpus estimates what your future SIP maturity may be worth in present-day purchasing power.

Can NRI invest in SIP in India?

Yes, NRIs can invest in SIP subject to KYC and fund house rules. This tool can be used as a sip calculator for nri india planning scenarios.

How is required SIP for target calculated?

The calculator uses iterative estimation to find a starting SIP amount that can reach your selected goal under current assumptions.

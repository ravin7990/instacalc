What is an SIP Calculator?

A SIP Calculator – also known as a Systematic Investment Plan Calculator – is an online tool designed to help investors forecast the value of their investments over time.

To use the calculator, simply input the following:

  • ✅ Monthly investment amount (e.g., ₹5,000)
  • ✅ Investment duration in months or years (e.g., 10 years)
  • ✅ Expected annual return rate (e.g., 12%)

The calculator then reveals your total estimated returns—making it easier to plan ahead using a sip return rate calculator.

How Does an SIP Calculator Work?

If you're curious about the math behind it, here’s the formula used by most SIP calculators:

FV = P × ((1 + r)^n - 1) / r × (1 + r)

Where:

  • P = Monthly SIP amount
  • r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12)
  • n = Total number of months (sip calculator months)
  • FV = Future Value of your investment

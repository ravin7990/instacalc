What is an Age Calculator?

The Age Calculator is a free tool that lets you calculate your age using date of birth. You simply enter your birth date, and it shows:

  • ✅ Your age in years, months, and days
  • ✅ Days left until your next birthday
  • ✅ Age on a past or future date
  • ✅ Day of the week you were born

It’s one of the easiest ways to calculate age online by date of birth perfect for all age-related calculations.

How Does the Age Calculator Work?

Our calculator net age tool works by calculating the difference between two dates:

  1. 1️⃣ Enter your date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY)
  2. 2️⃣ Select the "as on" date (default is today)
  3. 3️⃣ Hit "Calculate" and see your full age breakdown

It accurately handles leap years, month lengths, and future/past age lookups.

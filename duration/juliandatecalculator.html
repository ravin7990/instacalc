What is julian date and what is julian day number?

Julian date is a continuous day count, while julian day number is the integer day value used for date arithmetic and conversion.

How does julian date work compared with gregorian date?

Gregorian dates use year-month-day format. Julian date uses a continuous numeric count, which is easier for calculations across long date ranges.

What does a 5 digit julian date mean on products?

Usually it means YYDDD where YY is year and DDD is day number in that year. For example, 26120 maps to the 120th day of 2026.

What is modified julian date and why is it used?

Modified julian date is julian date minus 2400000.5, which keeps numbers smaller for technical and astronomical systems.

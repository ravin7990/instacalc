🕰️ What Is a Julian Date?

A Julian Date is a continuous count of days since January 1, 4713 BC. For example, the Julian Date for April 10, 2025, is 2460473.

It’s essential for fields that require accurate time tracking, like space exploration or data logging. That’s why a Julian Day Converter is still valuable today.

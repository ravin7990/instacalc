Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and explain clearly how data is handled on InstaCalc.in. This page describes what we collect, what we do not collect, and how third-party services are used.

Last Updated: February 11, 2025

Policy Highlights

InstaCalc is designed to be simple, privacy-conscious, and transparent.

  • No account is required to use calculators.
  • No personal financial data is requested for calculator use.
  • Analytics may use anonymized traffic information.

Welcome to InstaCalc.in (referred to as "the Website"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our tools, including financial and utility calculators. By using the Website, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

InstaCalc.in does not require registration or login for its public tools. We do not intentionally collect personal or sensitive data from calculator usage.

  • Personal details such as name, email, or phone number (unless you contact us directly).
  • Financial account details, card data, or banking credentials.
  • Persistent personal tracking data for profile building.

2. How We Use Information

Because we do not collect personal calculator input as identifiable user data, there is generally no personal data to store, resell, or share for calculator operations.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use analytics tools to understand overall website performance, such as traffic volume and popular pages. This may include anonymized technical details like:

  • Browser type and version.
  • Device or operating system category.
  • Referring source and pages visited.

These insights are used only to improve usability and reliability.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies for behavioral advertising in this policy version. If functional cookies are used in the future, they will be limited to essential website behavior or tool experience improvements.

5. Data Security

Although calculator usage is designed to avoid collecting sensitive personal data, we still apply reasonable technical measures to protect the site infrastructure and maintain service integrity.

6. Links to External Websites

Our pages may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. Please review their policies before sharing personal details on those sites.

7. Children's Privacy

InstaCalc.in is not specifically directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the public calculator workflow.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or service changes. Updates will appear on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

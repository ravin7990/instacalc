Looking to turn your investment into a regular income? The SWP Calculator is here to help! Also known as a Systematic Withdrawal Plan Calculator, it shows how much you can withdraw monthly without exhausting your funds too quickly.

What is an SWP Calculator?

A Systematic Withdrawal Plan Calculator estimates how long your investment will last based on a few inputs:

  • ✔️ Your initial investment amount
  • ✔️ The fixed monthly withdrawal
  • ✔️ Expected rate of return
  • ✔️ Total withdrawal duration

Unlike lump sum withdrawals, an SWP lets you withdraw a steady amount regularly. Your remaining investment keeps earning returns, which is the core benefit of using a SWP plan calculator. 📈

How Does an SWP Calculator Work?

This best SWP calculator gives you a detailed picture of:

  • ✅ The monthly amount you can withdraw
  • ✅ How long your funds will last based on returns
  • ✅ The balance remaining after each withdrawal

How to Use This SWP Calculator Online:

  1. 1️⃣ Enter your initial investment (e.g., ₹10,00,000)

  3. 3️⃣ Choose the expected return rate (e.g., 8% annually)
  4. 4️⃣ Select the withdrawal duration (e.g., 10 years)
  5. 5️⃣ Click "Calculate" to get your results instantly

An SWP is a facility offered by mutual funds that allows investors to withdraw a fixed amount of money at regular intervals (monthly, quarterly, etc.) from their investment.
The SWP Calculator estimates the amount you can withdraw regularly based on your investment corpus, expected rate of return, and withdrawal frequency. It helps you plan your withdrawals without depleting your investment too quickly.
You need to provide:
  • Investment Corpus: The total amount you have invested.
  • Expected Annual Return: The rate of return you expect from your investment.
  • Withdrawal Frequency: How often you want to withdraw (monthly, quarterly, etc.).
  • Withdrawal Amount: The fixed amount you wish to withdraw regularly.

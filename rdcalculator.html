How this recurring deposit maturity calculator works

This RD calculator with interest simulates month-by-month deposits and compounds earnings based on your selected frequency. It supports step-up contributions, beginning/end-of-month deposit timing, and target-based planning.


Formula reference: the model uses effective monthly growth from selected annual RD rate, then accumulates each monthly instalment over the remaining tenure.


Popular RD intents covered: rd calculator monthly deposit, rd maturity amount calculator, rd return calculator online, recurring deposit return calculator, rd principal and interest calculator.

How to calculate RD maturity amount?

Enter monthly deposit, RD interest rate, and tenure. The calculator compounds deposits month by month and shows maturity value, interest earned, and post-tax results.

Can this work as an RD calculator with TDS deduction?

Yes. Use the tax slab control to estimate post-tax RD maturity and RD interest income after deductions.

What is the difference between RD and FD for savings goals?

RD builds discipline through monthly instalments, while FD is usually a one-time deposit. For recurring monthly savings, RD is generally more suitable.

How does senior citizen RD extra rate impact maturity?

Senior citizen mode adds an extra rate boost and recalculates maturity value, interest earned, and required monthly deposit for your target.

Is RD interest taxable in India and how does TDS apply?

RD interest is taxable as per slab rates. TDS deduction rules depend on current limits and declarations. Use the tax slab setting here to estimate net maturity and interest income.

Can we break RD before maturity or renew it after completion?

Most banks allow premature closure with penalties and also allow renewal after maturity. Check bank-specific policy before opening or renewing your RD account online.

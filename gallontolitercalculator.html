FAQ

How many liters are in 1 gallon?

A US liquid gallon is 3.785 liters and an imperial gallon is 4.546 liters. Results differ because they are not the same gallon unit. Always select the right system when converting milk, water, fuel, or tank capacity data.

Is 4 liters the same as 1 gallon?

No. Four liters is close but not exact. It is slightly above one US gallon and below one imperial gallon. If precision matters, use the calculator and avoid rounding one gallon to exactly four liters.

How many liters are in a US gallon vs Imperial gallon?

US liquid gallon: 3.785 liters. Imperial gallon: 4.546 liters. Since imperial gallons are larger, gallon-to-liter values are higher for the same gallon number in UK mode.

Why is a UK gallon different from a US gallon?

They were defined from different historical standards. As a result, UK and US gallon sizes remain different, and direct gallon comparisons are not reliable without converting to liters first.

How do you calculate gallons to liters?

Multiply gallons by the correct factor: US liquid 3.785411784, US dry 4.40488377086, or imperial 4.54609. This is the gallon to liter conversion formula used in this calculator.

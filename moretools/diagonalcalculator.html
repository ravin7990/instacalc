FAQ

What is diagonal of a rectangle?

It is the straight distance from one corner to the opposite corner.

What is diagonal of square formula?

Diagonal equals side multiplied by square root of 2.

How to measure TV screen diagonal?

Measure only the visible display area corner-to-corner, not bezel.

How to square a room using diagonal?

Compare expected diagonal from length and width with measured corner-to-corner distance.
